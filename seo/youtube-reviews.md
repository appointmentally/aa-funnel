# Video reviews → YouTube (paste-ready upload copy)

Your client video reviews are gold sitting on Instagram where Google barely sees them. Re-upload each to **YouTube** (free, your own channel "Appointment Ally"). Why: YouTube is the #2 search engine, Google surfaces YouTube in results + AI Overviews, and ChatGPT/Perplexity cite video transcripts. This turns proof you already have into indexable, AI-citable assets.

**Setup once:** create a YouTube channel "Appointment Ally", add a banner + the description below, link yourapptally.com.
